Rules

Normal Mode

  • Roll the dice to accumulate points.
  • Press the hold button to save them and swap turn.
  • If you role a 1 you lose your current score and your turn.
  • Reach 100 points before your opponent to win the game.

Hard Mode

  • In hard mode you play with two dices
  • Win the game by reaching 200 points before your opponent.
  • If you role a 2, a 5 or a 8, with the total sum of your roled dices, you lose your turn and your current score.
  • However if you role a sum of 7, you will be given the chance to diminishe your opponent score with the total of your acumulated current score.
